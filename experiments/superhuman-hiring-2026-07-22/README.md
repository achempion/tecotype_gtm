# Superhuman hiring research source pack

Captured: 2026-07-22

Source: [Superhuman Platform Inc jobs on Ashby](https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/Superhuman%20Platform%20Inc)

## Contents

- `open-roles.json`: all 76 role cards visible on the public board at capture time, including source URLs.
- `job-descriptions/`: 16 source-linked evidence notes covering marketing and adjacent growth, product, engineering, and operations roles.
- [`../../superhuman-hiring-marketing-analysis.md`](../../superhuman-hiring-marketing-analysis.md): synthesis and Tecotype implications.

## Selection

The 16 detailed records include the explicit Core Marketing, Growth, and Managed Marketing roles most relevant to the question, plus adjacent roles that reveal acquisition operations, product-led growth, suite cross-activation, enterprise buyer experience, and experimentation capacity.

The notes paraphrase the public postings and preserve their URLs. Follow a source link for the complete current job description. This avoids treating a copied snapshot as authoritative after the live role changes or closes.

## Interpretation rules

- Hiring indicates intended capability, not shipped programs or proven results.
- Company-wide suite roles are separated from Superhuman Mail-specific roles.
- Absence of an open role is weak evidence because existing employees or agencies may cover the function.
- The capture is a point-in-time snapshot and should be dated when cited.
