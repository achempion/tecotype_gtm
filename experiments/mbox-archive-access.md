# Local MBOX archive access

Status: Discovery only — not shipped or committed.

People leaving Gmail can struggle to open or search large MBOX exports. Possible
path: MBOX search or support guide → free local viewer → evaluate Tecotype for
active mail.

Signal: DataForSEO estimates 1,900 US monthly searches for `how to open mbox
file` and 1,600 for `mbox viewer`; a
[Google support question](https://support.google.com/accounts/thread/173880997/how-can-i-open-mbox-files-from-google-takeout?hl=en)
records 133 people with the same problem.
