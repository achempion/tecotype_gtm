# Gmail search misses known mail

Status: Discovery only — not shipped or committed.

Gmail users can search for mail they know exists yet see few or no results until
switching from relevance to recency. Possible path: useful diagnosis/fix →
Tecotype local-search demonstration → product evaluation.

Signal: DataForSEO estimates 260 US monthly searches for `gmail search not
working` at modeled difficulty 0 (task
`07171956-2101-0607-0000-859b3d83a1b8`); a
[Google community case](https://support.google.com/mail/thread/406655610/gmail-search-does-not-find-content-in-email-or-subject-line?hl=en)
documents the 2026 relevance-order failure and fix.
