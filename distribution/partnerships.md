# Provider relationships and distribution

Purpose: provider relationship and distribution rules. No provider has
endorsed, recommended, distributed, or committed to distribute Tecotype.

The canonical acquisition verdict is
[next-acquisition-funnel.md](../next-acquisition-funnel.md). This document
defines the narrower relationship and claim boundaries.

## Terms

- **Compatible:** Tecotype can connect through a supported protocol.
  Compatibility is not provider testing or endorsement.
- **Provider-tested:** the provider has verified named connection facts for a
  specified Tecotype version. This is not a recommendation.
- **Recommended:** the provider independently publishes a recommendation or
  client listing.
- **Distribution:** the provider permanently introduces people with no prior
  Tecotype use before installation, and the attributable first-use-to-paid
  path operates.

A help article, logo, backlink, marketplace listing, cumulative page counter,
or large provider customer base does not by itself establish distribution.

## Minimum relationship

Before any provider-specific product work, require:

- the exact permanent pre-install placement;
- a direct link to Tecotype's normal signed download;
- editorial, security-review, support, duration, and removal terms; and
- aggregate measurement without mailbox or per-user referral data.

Traffic, paid-bridge, and experiment requirements live only in the
[current acquisition decision](../next-acquisition-funnel.md).

## Product and privacy boundary

- Tecotype signs, licenses, distributes, supports, and updates one normal
  application.
- The provider may host a link, factual setup guide, or small content-free
  helper. It may not repackage or update the desktop application.
- Mailbox credentials, tokens, content, addresses, domains, messages, searches,
  and derived mailbox data never enter acquisition systems.
- No partner SDK, managed updater, provider-specific desktop binary, or custom
  licensing server is required.
- Removing a listing cannot disable, relicense, or strand an installed
  Tecotype application.

Mailbox traffic still travels directly between the application and the user's
email provider. “Local” must not be misrepresented as “email never leaves the
device.”

## Commercial boundary

No provider-specific entitlement, reseller plan, revenue share, white-label
edition, or team offer is decided here. A future proposal must preserve
[pricing](../pricing.md) and include every partner-specific cost in the
[acquisition gate](../acquisition/readme.md#one-hard-gate).

## Claim rules

- Name the exact tested version, date, scope, and limitations.
- Obtain permission before using a provider logo or quote.
- Never turn compatibility into “official client,” “approved,” “endorsed,” or
  “recommended.”
- Never turn a provider link into “distribution” before the strict definition
  is met.
- Remove or correct stale provider claims promptly.
