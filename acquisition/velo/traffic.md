## Live query checks

Three current top-10 Google result pages were requested.

### `velo mail email client`

Observed:

- Velo homepage at absolute position 1;
- GitHub repository at absolute position 3;
- the founder’s second Reddit post at absolute position 4;
- a third-party YouTube Short at absolute position 5;
- an unrelated “Velo Email” Chrome extension also present.

The branded query has good entity coverage across owned and earned surfaces. It also has brand collision.

### `open source desktop email client`

Velo was absent from the top 10. Established category pages and products such as Thunderbird occupied the result set.

### `keyboard first email client`

Velo was absent from the top 10. The current landing promise has not translated into visible top-10 category capture in this market snapshot.

Live SERPs are observations at one date and market. They do not establish historical ranks or visits.

## On-page search readiness

Observed strengths:

- descriptive title and meta description;
- canonical URL;
- index/follow directive;
- Open Graph and Twitter metadata;
- SoftwareApplication and FAQ structured data;
- robots access;
- text naming desktop email, open source, keyboard use, privacy, Gmail, IMAP, and platforms.

Observed constraints:

- only one indexable public page in the sitemap;
- no dedicated provider, platform, comparison, feature, documentation, release, or trust pages on the domain;
- stale sitemap `lastmod`;
- structured-data version `1.0` conflicts with public version `0.4.21`;
- broad and absolute privacy language creates a trust risk if surfaced in snippets;
- “Velo” is used by many unrelated products.

## Search interpretation

### Observed

Velo owns its exact product-plus-category query well enough to place the domain, repository, social discussion, and video in one result page.

### Inference

The launch and repository probably generated branded demand. The current domain has not built enough topical surface or authority to intercept broader open-source or keyboard-first email demand.

### Unknown

- Google Search Console impressions and clicks;
- country and device mix;
- query-to-download behavior;
- branded-search lift during the launch;
- ranking or traffic history between February and July;
- activation or retention from organic search.

## Implication for Tecotype

Velo is not evidence that category SEO drove launch adoption. It is evidence that an attention event can create a useful branded result page.

Tecotype should defer broad search acquisition until the release gate is complete. Afterward, a small, truthful page system can test:

1. keyboard-first email for Mac;
2. local email search;
3. Gmail and IMAP workflow pages;
4. privacy architecture with exact data-flow boundaries;
5. platform requirements, setup, and signed-download trust.

Each page needs a source-preserving CTA and activation cohort. Rankings or modeled ETV alone should not determine continuation.
