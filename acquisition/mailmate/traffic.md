## Current SERPs

### `mailmate email client`

The homepage ranked first organically. An independent essay, Reddit review,
older user article, official manual, YouTube playlist, pricing page, download
page, and MacUpdate result filled the rest of the observed top results.

This is a strong brand/category result page for someone already looking for
MailMate. It provides both official and independent evaluation.

### `keyboard email client mac`

MailMate's own site did not appear in the requested top 10. Results included
Reddit, an independent comparison, Apple support, Mimestream, Airmail, and
roundups. One roundup snippet mentioned MailMate at position eight in a list.

The product's central message therefore does not currently translate into
visible first-page ownership of this checked non-brand query.

## Paid search and advertising

The Labs snapshot had no paid rankings. A Google Ads Transparency advertiser
lookup for `mailmate email client` and a domain creative lookup for
`freron.com` returned `40102` no-result task statuses in the requested US
coverage.

That is no observed result, not proof that MailMate never advertised. The
founder's historical statements and the current page are more consistent with
owned and community distribution than a scaled paid channel.

## Acquisition interpretation

MailMate is discoverable after somebody knows its name. The public data does
not support a claim that it systematically acquires from broad Mac email,
keyboard, security, or search queries.

For Tecotype, the useful lesson is not “ignore search.” It is that a narrow
reputation can survive with little generic visibility, while growth remains
constrained. Tecotype should test one job-close page and its downstream
activation before investing in a large content inventory.
