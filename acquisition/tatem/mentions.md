# Brand mentions

Tatem is an ambiguous name and was used for several products. Broad mention counts are low-value until each page is dated and classified.

## DataForSEO result

`content_analysis/search/live` searched for the exact phrase `"tatem"` plus `email` on 2026-07-17.

- DataForSEO reported 1,018 matching documents.
- The request returned 50 rows.
- Most rows concerned people named Tatem, contact pages, or unrelated companies.

The total is a query-match count, not 1,018 relevant mentions. It is not used to support an acquisition conclusion.

## Launch and content checks

No indexed Tatem Email launch was found on Product Hunt, Hacker News, or BetaList during this research. This is a search dead end, not proof that no launch existed.

The current [writing page](https://tatem.com/writing) is a brand/style guide. The [changelog](https://tatem.com/changelog) contains a short product-update run ending in September 2025. No repeatable email-era editorial or non-brand SEO program was found.

## Verdict

- **Most useful:** direct community discussions with visible interest and friction.
- **Useful as presence:** dedicated email-era gallery and third-party social pages.
- **Exclude:** sources tied to older Tatem products.
- **Inconclusive:** broad mention counts, missing launch results, impressions, clicks, activation, and payment.
