# Tatem history

Tatem reused one brand and domain across materially different products and offers. This timeline prevents project-management evidence from being mistaken for email-client acquisition evidence.

Dates are based on public pages and archive captures. A change observed between two captures occurred somewhere inside that interval; it should not be assigned a more precise date without another source.

## Interpretation rules

- A backlink first seen while Tatem sold project management is **legacy or unknown** until its referring page is checked.
- A current page can establish what remains public, but not what drove growth during 2023–2025.
- A removed message or reversed offer was **replaced**, not necessarily unsuccessful.
- A public complaint is evidence of friction and perception, not a measured churn rate.
- The “10,000+” homepage claim is first-party and undefined; it cannot be assigned to a channel or treated as active users.
- The Wander announcement establishes that the founder and team joined Wander, but does not disclose which corporate, product, brand, domain, or audience assets changed ownership.
