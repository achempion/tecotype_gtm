# Referrals, communities, and backlinks

The domain-level backlink index mixes Tatem’s operations, project-management, and email products. It is useful for finding candidate discovery surfaces, but the raw total is not an email-acquisition metric.

These totals span multiple products and years. They are not visits, distinct people, signups, or evidence that a channel worked.

## Email-community discovery

The strongest public email-era path is an [August 2024 discussion about Superhuman’s price increase](https://www.reddit.com/r/SuperhumanEmail/comments/1f2w0wx/leaving_superhuman_after_recent_price_increaes/):

1. People described price and workflow pain.
2. Tatem was recommended in the discussion.
3. Several participants said they joined its waitlist.
4. Tatem’s founder participated and described a plan to expand access through existing-user referrals.
5. Other participants reported long waits, stale release information, or doubts about the product.
6. The founder returned in July 2025 to announce the rebuilt, self-serve product.

A [Superhuman lifetime-access discussion](https://www.reddit.com/r/SuperhumanEmail/comments/151fpqb/superhuman_lifetime_subscription/) also contains a Tatem recommendation, a company response, and later reports of long waits without onboarding.

This evidence supports:

```text
Existing incumbent pain
→ community discovery
→ recommendation or disclosed founder response
→ self-reported waitlist signup
```

It does not support:

```text
waitlist
→ access
→ activation
→ retained or paying customer
```

Recommendations in other old threads may have created additional discovery, but affiliation, impressions, and outcomes are unknown. Similar timing alone is not enough to call them organic, coordinated, or deceptive.

## What appears productive or costly

**Plausibly productive at the interest stage:** The discussion path matched a high-intent trigger and included visible statements of waitlist signup. Public evidence does not identify which recommendation or founder action caused them.

**Costly:** The same channel preserved public evidence of people waiting for months, feeling unable to access the product, and questioning its existence.

**Presence only:** Design galleries clearly listed Tatem Email and created links. No public source connects them to qualified sessions or product use.

**Contaminated:** Reviews, Wefunder, many early backlinks, and founder coverage belong to older products.

## Backlink history boundary

The backlink-history request returned 54 monthly observations beginning in January 2022. Because the first reliable email-homepage capture is August 2023, early growth cannot be credited to the email product. Later totals still contain persistent legacy links.

For example, backlink counts increased around the email pivot and during 2024, but timing alone cannot identify the cause. A backlink spike is a prompt to inspect sources, not a conversion result.

## Repeatable candidates for Tecotype

Two patterns are worth testing after the appropriate prerequisites:

1. Helpful, disclosed participation where people already express a specific email problem.
2. Tagged submissions to curated design and product sites after Tecotype has a polished, accurate page and source-to-activation measurement.

The first has qualitative evidence of interest and an equally important warning about premature demand. The second has evidence of placement only.
