## Mention-query collision

Content Analysis returned 100 of 2,543 rows for `Airo Mail`. The leading rows
were unrelated to the product: GoDaddy Airo, radiotherapy organizations, vape
products, business names, and generic occurrences of “mail.” The result is not
a usable brand-mention count.

This is an important distribution constraint. Exact-domain and exact-product
URLs are more reliable than brand-word monitoring, and paid brand tools could
waste budget on false matches.

## Owned content

Airo publishes:

- audience pages for ADHD, consultants, founders, lawyers, and executive
  assistants;
- direct comparisons with Superhuman, Shortwave, and Spark; and
- broad iPhone, Gmail organization, and workflow guides.

The [iPhone email-app comparison](https://www.airo.email/blog/best-email-apps-iphone)
discloses founder bias and positions AutoFile as the differentiator. This is a
responsible comparison format, but current Labs data does not show it creating
meaningful US search visibility.

## Interpretation for Tecotype

**Observed:** exact-brand results are coherent because the homepage and two
stores occupy three visible results.

**Inferred:** audience pages and comparisons are intended to turn a narrow
message into search entry points.

**Unobservable:** search clicks, demo starts, activation, payments, and
retention.

Tecotype should test one audience-specific desktop page at a time and measure
downstream outcomes. A large role-page inventory before indexed demand or
conversion would reproduce Airo’s surface area without evidence.
