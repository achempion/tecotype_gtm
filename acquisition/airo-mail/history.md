## Founder and problem history

On [Our Story](https://www.airo.email/our_story), Charles Guo says he spent
almost two years building Airo after ten years at Google and Airbnb, including
G Suite work. He describes the original bet as making repetitive email filing
feel like a review game with a visible safety threshold.

One captured/indexed homepage retains AutoFile but leads with a different
commercial frame: a solo founder protecting customer, lead, investor, invoice,
and follow-up work. The later direct fetch instead leads with mobile inbox
organization, packages, trips, and Agenda. The research cannot prove whether
this is an experiment, cache, conditional assignment, or rapid repositioning.

## Audience and message variants

Current owned surfaces expose at least three related frames:

1. **ADHD and repetitive decisions:** founder story and role page.
2. **Mobile inbox organization:** direct homepage, App Store, and Google Play
   copy about packages, trips, Agenda, and AutoFile.
3. **Solo-founder operating inbox:** captured/indexed homepage with Plate,
   follow-ups, tasks, leads, customers, and investors.

The third is the clearest willingness-to-pay frame because it relates email to
commercial outcomes. The direct page and stores remain consumer-oriented,
which may expand reach while making the audience and price harder to compare.

## Price and offer evolution

The current public surfaces are not synchronized:

- App Store exposes Personal $9.99 and Pro $29.99 monthly, plus $96 and $300
  annual purchases.
- Direct homepage exposes Personal at $8/month and Pro at $25/month billed
  annually, plus Business custom.
- Captured/indexed founder content emphasizes Pro and Team.
- Captured Product structured data includes a $30 Pro offer.

This could be an audience experiment or a move between consumer/mobile and
founder-specific professional segmentation, but the direction, assignment,
switch date, and plan eligibility cannot be proven.

## Distribution changes

The durable discovery stack has expanded from a site/waitlist to:

- a web application and, in one observed variant, a live-demo route;
- Apple and Google stores;
- founder LinkedIn and TikTok links;
- role pages;
- comparison and educational content; and
- a disclosed founder presence in a relevant Reddit conversation.

The surface expansion has not produced visible search or referral scale:
DataForSEO found two keywords and one indexed referring domain. Store evidence
also remains tiny.

## Tecotype lesson

A precise audience can make a high price intelligible, and a no-sign-in demo
can bridge message to evaluation. Airo's simultaneous or cached variants also
show why a segment test needs controlled assignment and a canonical offer.
Repositioning only becomes distribution when source-level activation and
payment improve. Tecotype should maintain one current price and platform truth
across metadata, landing pages, catalogs, reviewer briefs, and checkout.
