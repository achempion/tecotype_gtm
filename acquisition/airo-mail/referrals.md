# Airo Mail referrals and public discovery

Captured: **2026-07-17**

## App Store

The [US App Store listing](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/airo-mail/id6747727414)
showed:

- one rating at 5.0;
- free acquisition with in-app purchases;
- Personal at $9.99/month and Pro at $29.99/month;
- Personal annual at $96 and Pro annual at $300;
- a long visible update history; and
- iPhone distribution, with Apple Silicon Mac compatibility listed but “not
  verified for macOS” in the store surface.

Apple’s public
[lookup API](https://itunes.apple.com/lookup?id=6747727414&country=us) reported
an original release date of July 24, 2025 and version 1.0.243 released July 15,
2026 at capture time. One rating is evidence of one public response, not broad
satisfaction or adoption.

## Google Play

The [Google Play listing](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=email.airo.rena&hl=en_US)
showed:

- **10+ downloads**;
- free acquisition with $10–$300 in-app purchases in embedded metadata;
- version 1.0.246;
- updated July 17, 2026; and
- Gmail / Google Workspace, 14-day no-card trial, and privacy positioning.

The store’s data-safety section says the app may share app information,
performance, and device identifiers with third parties and may collect personal
information, messages, and other data. The publisher’s detailed security page
provides the operational context. Ten-plus downloads are a request/install-band
signal, not unique activated or paid users.

Mobile acquisition itself is out of scope for Tecotype. The relevant lesson is
that permanent platform listings can occupy branded search and give prospects
an independent metadata and review surface.

## Founder surfaces

[Charles Guo’s LinkedIn profile](https://www.linkedin.com/in/charles-guo)
showed 486 followers and current Airo activity, including posts about email
triage and shareable email links. The homepage also links to his TikTok account.
No public source provides reach, clicks, trials, or payments from those posts.

The founder’s [Our Story](https://www.airo.email/our_story) page is an owned
referral asset: the Google/Airbnb background and personal problem explain why
the interaction differs from a normal inbox.

## Disclosed Reddit participation

In a relevant
[r/AiForSmallBusiness thread](https://www.reddit.com/r/AiForSmallBusiness/comments/1skfl2p/trying_to_find_the_best_ai_for_email_organization/),
a participant asked for Gmail organization, tasks, and follow-up tracking. A
commenter disclosed “I build in this space,” named Airo Mail, described it as a
Gmail replacement across iOS, Android, and web, and explained its batch-review
model.

The disclosure and problem-specific explanation are good practice. The
captured post showed a score of zero and no public source connects the comment
to a visit or trial. It is one relevant founder conversation, not an acquisition
engine.

## Tecotype relevance

Tecotype should adapt the permanent-discovery principle to desktop stores,
package catalogs, specialist reviewers, and durable comparison pages, not build
mobile apps. Every listing needs source tags or a measurable referral boundary
and must be evaluated through activated paid use.
