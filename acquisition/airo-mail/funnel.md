## Offer and friction

Across the two homepage states, Airo provides:

- a live demo labeled “no sign-in” in the captured founder variant, but not in
  the later direct fetch;
- 14 free days with all Pro features;
- no credit card required;
- one email account during trial;
- $8/month billed annually for Personal on the direct page;
- $25/month billed annually for Pro;
- up to ten Gmail accounts on Pro; and
- a custom Business or Team path, depending on the public state.

The FAQ says an unpaid account pauses after trial until the user subscribes.
The no-card trial avoids immediate payment friction, while the $300 annual
commitment creates a strong qualification threshold.

## Price across surfaces

| Surface | Observed offer |
| --- | --- |
| Direct homepage, annual state | Personal $8/month; Pro $25/month; Business custom |
| Captured founder variant | Pro-focused offer; Team custom; no-sign-in demo |
| Captured structured offer | Pro $30; likely monthly, but toggle not used |
| App Store | Personal $9.99/month, Pro $29.99/month, annual $96 / $300 |
| Google Play metadata | In-app purchases $10–$300 |

The professional annual price is visible and unusually high for the observed
adoption signals. Different plan names and entry prices across web and stores
may reflect channel packaging or a recent repositioning. The buyer cannot infer
that without explanation.

## Activation hypothesis

Airo’s variants provide unusually concrete activation clues:

1. connect a Gmail account;
2. see Plate identify a reply, task, or follow-up in the founder variant, or
   see Agenda surface work in the direct variant;
3. review an AutoFile batch;
4. approve or veto suggestions; and
5. end with an important-message queue, action plan, or cleaner inbox.

These are publisher-demonstrated jobs, not observed user behavior. The page
does not publish trial starts, connected accounts, time to first reviewed
action, classification corrections, day-seven return, or paid conversion.

## Trust handoff

The homepage’s broad privacy reassurance links to a detailed
[security page](https://www.airo.email/security). That page explains that Airo
stores message bodies on its backend while accounts are active and sends data
to Google Vertex AI for inference. A buyer can therefore evaluate the actual
tradeoff before OAuth.

The handoff is stronger than vague reassurance, though “most Gmail data stays
on your device” can be misread without following the link.

## What is attributable

No public Airo evidence connects a named discovery source to:

- demo start or completion;
- trial start;
- Gmail connection;
- first Plate or AutoFile action;
- voluntary return;
- subscription; or
- renewal.

One App Store rating and 10+ Google Play downloads are downstream public
signals but are not attributable to the homepage, founder content, search, or
another source.

## Tecotype adaptation

The most relevant marketing path is:

```text
specialist review, segment page, or high-intent query
→ exact desktop job and audience
→ faithful no-account demonstration
→ current platform and price
→ source-preserved evaluation
→ activation
→ payment and renewal
```

The demo should show real product behavior and the exact privacy boundary,
without requiring mailbox access merely to understand the promise.
