## Current SERPs

### `newton mail`

Newton's homepage ranked first organically. The Apple App Store, Wikipedia, a
Todoist integration page, YouTube, Reddit, TechRadar, X, and Medium also
appeared in the checked results.

This is a useful branded result page because it provides owned, store,
integration, editorial, and community evaluation. It also exposes shutdown
history and support sentiment alongside the official story.

### `newton mail alternative`

Newton's own site was absent from the checked top results. Results included:

- a Reddit replacement discussion;
- [Mailbird's alternative page](https://www.getmailbird.com/best-alternative-to-newton-mail/);
- Drag;
- Spark-related or other email-client alternatives;
- BoldAugust;
- YouTube;
- Flow-e;
- Spike; and
- SourceForge.

The query is a permanent acquisition asset for other clients. It was created
by Newton's discontinuity and remains useful to competitors even while Newton
is live.

### `best email client for professionals`

Newton's site was absent from the checked top ten. Missive, PCMag, Reddit,
Mailbird, Efficient, Zapier, and SweetBits occupied visible results.

The homepage's stated business/professional audience is therefore not matched
by observed ownership of this commercial-category query.

### `calm email client`

Newton's site was absent. Results were dominated by the Calm meditation brand
and support pages, with eM Client, PCMag, and a Chrome extension also visible.

“Calm” can be an effective emotional outcome, but this check does not support
it as a standalone acquisition keyword. It needs a concrete email job or
category around it.

## Paid search and advertising

The Labs overview showed no paid keywords or ETV. A Google Ads advertiser
lookup for `newton mail` and a creative lookup for `newtonhq.com` returned
`40102` no-search-results statuses in the requested US coverage.

Both tasks were billed. The result means no creative was returned for those
specific requests, not that Newton has never advertised.

## Acquisition interpretation

Newton can still capture someone who remembers Newton or CloudMagic. The
current public evidence does not show a comparable route for someone searching
for a professional, calm, scheduled, collaborative, or cross-platform email
client without already knowing the name.

For Tecotype:

- use calm as the emotional layer around a concrete job;
- build one job-close page before a large content inventory;
- create a truthful alternative or comparison page only when the match is
  relevant;
- date historical proof;
- preserve source identity into activation and payment; and
- treat brand rank as navigation, not net-new distribution.
