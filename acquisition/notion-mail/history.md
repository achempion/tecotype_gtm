## Positioning evolution

The preview message was liberation from the old inbox. Launch shifted to "The inbox that thinks like you," emphasizing personalized views and AI organization. The shutdown explanation shifts the strategic center again—from an inbox interface to agent-mediated email work.

```text
waitlist and inbox reinvention
    -> free personalized Gmail client
    -> web/Mac plus iOS
    -> agent-centered company narrative
    -> scheduled inbox closure and migration
```

The dates and public messages are observed. The causal explanation is only partly reported.

## Shutdown rationale: what can and cannot be said

[TechCrunch's shutdown report](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/25/notion-mail-shuts-down-amid-agent-takeover/) says Notion found that more than half of users manage emails without opening the inbox and chose to focus on agents. This supports the conclusion that Notion is reallocating attention away from the inbox surface.

It does **not** disclose:

- total or active users;
- the measurement window or definition of "manage";
- whether those users had ever adopted the inbox deeply;
- retention by launch channel;
- Notion Mail revenue or paid-plan lift;
- whether product quality, strategy, cost, or another factor caused the closure.

The shutdown therefore cannot responsibly be reduced to "the product failed" or attributed to one cause.

## Skiff context

[Skiff's migration page](https://skiff.com/data-migration) says its services
sunset after the Notion acquisition, that Skiff accounts and data were not
converted into Notion accounts, and that mail forwarding continued only for a
limited period. TechCrunch's [Notion Mail launch
report](https://techcrunch.com/2025/04/15/notion-releases-its-ai-driven-email-inbox/)
says Notion Mail's infrastructure came from Skiff. Together, these sources
support reported team and infrastructure continuity while explicitly ruling
out account conversion. They do not establish unchanged source code, shared
user data, or a direct Skiff-to-Notion-Mail migration.

Two email-related service transitions under the wider Notion story can
heighten buyer sensitivity to durability. This is a marketing trust
consideration, not evidence about acquisition performance.

## Historical surface debt

The current product page and some platform "soon" language still reflect launch-era acquisition. The authoritative shutdown outcome lives elsewhere. A lifecycle transition should update the highest-ranking acquisition page, store, help, comparison assets, and partner messages together.
