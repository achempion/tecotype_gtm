# Notion Mail acquisition and migration funnel

This maps public marketing and official transition guidance only. No download, app, login, account, Gmail connection, mailbox, form, export action, or checkout was used.

## Acquisition funnel before the shutdown announcement

```text
Notion release / waitlist / press / brand search / community
    -> notion.com/product/mail
    -> free web or macOS start; later iOS
    -> Notion account + Gmail OAuth
    -> custom view / auto-label / first useful email action
    -> retained inbox use
    -> possible Notion-plan conversion
```

| Stage | Public evidence | Attribution limit |
|---|---|---|
| Preview | Official 2024 waitlist and archived form | Waitlist size and source mix unknown |
| Discovery | Release posts, launch press, backlinks, branded search, store | Reach and incrementality unknown |
| Offer | Free Gmail client; AI free with limits and broader paid access reported at launch | Product-page-to-plan economics unknown |
| Continuation | Web/macOS at launch and later iOS | Routes were not entered |
| Connection | Notion account plus Gmail/Workspace requirements in help | OAuth completion unobserved |
| Activation | Custom inbox view, useful auto-label, or completed email action are plausible events | Notion did not publish an activation definition |
| Retention | Company later says more than half manage email without opening the inbox | Denominator, window, cohort, and causal meaning unknown |
| Payment | Broader Notion paid-plan access could remove AI limits | No source-level plan conversion or Mail-attributed revenue public |

The quoted "more than half" behavior, reported by [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/25/notion-mail-shuts-down-amid-agent-takeover/), could mean the inbox was bypassed, successfully substituted by agents, or both. It does not by itself prove poor acquisition, poor retention, or failed monetization.

## Current public funnel conflict

As of 2026-07-17:

```text
Branded search
    -> still-promotional product page
    -> "Get Notion Mail free"

Shutdown / migration search
    -> official help
    -> save or export Notion-Mail-only material
    -> return to Gmail or choose another workflow
```

The first route still acquires for a surface scheduled to close. The second is the authoritative lifecycle route. No CTA was followed, so warnings after the landing page are unknown.

## Exact announced migration outcome

Notion's official [shutdown guidance](https://www.notion.com/help/notion-mail-inbox-is-going-away-what-to-do-next) states:

- export/save access began 2026-06-25;
- 2026-09-21 is the final day to save material;
- on 2026-09-22, the iOS, desktop, and web inbox app will shut down;
- unsaved data that exists only in Notion Mail will be deleted;
- Gmail email remains in Gmail because of two-way sync;
- drafts, scheduled emails, snippets, and auto-label instructions should be saved;
- custom views and sorting do not transfer;
- snippet attachments require manual handling;
- reminders do not transfer;
- existing synced email databases and views remain but stop updating;
- Gmail AI Connector, existing mail blocks, and Gmail agent tools continue.

The article also says HIPAA users should have transitioned by 2026-06-30. That is Notion's published deadline, not legal advice.

## Funnel implication

The migration outcome creates a new high-intent job:

```text
Understand deadline
    -> identify what stays
    -> identify what must be saved
    -> select replacement workflow
    -> complete first useful action
    -> retain
    -> pay
```

A publisher can rank for the first three steps without creating distribution. The source must persist through the replacement workflow's activation and paid event before the route is business-attributable.

## Friction hypotheses

These are **inferences to test**, not measured findings:

- A stale signup page can reduce trust after a user encounters shutdown reporting.
- A simple "stays / save / does not transfer" matrix can outperform generic alternative copy.
- Existing Notion Mail users may value view customization but fear another product sunset.
- Migration pages published by competitors can capture intent before the shutdown date, but aggressive fear language may damage trust.
- Exact import claims will convert only if they match the real export artifacts and current release.

## What remains unobservable

Post-announcement starts, export usage, abandonment, migration destinations, active inbox users, agent-only users, plan conversion, cancellation, and channel-level economics are not public.
