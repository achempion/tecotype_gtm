feature architecture.

**Inferred:** the content program is deliberately targeting both broad support
queries and high-intent alternative queries.

**Unobservable:** organic visits, signup rate, activated users, payments, and
retention from any page.

For Tecotype, the relevant experiment is one high-intent page around an
observed switching job, not a broad AI-email content inventory. The page should
earn continuation only if its source-tagged visitors activate and pay.
