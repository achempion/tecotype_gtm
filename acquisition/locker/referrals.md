# Locker referrals, communities, and backlinks

Research date: **2026-07-17**

Scope: **current email product only**

`drop.inbox.locker` is a separate encrypted-file-transfer product by the same
maker. Links to it are excluded from conclusions about Locker Email.

## Executive finding

The only public referral path with a visible human response is one founder
Reddit post. DataForSEO found no credible email-product backlink or content
mention. The raw domain totals are inflated by:

- Links created before the current email product launched.
- Generic URL-report pages.
- Obvious backlink-network spam.
- Links to the separate Locker Drop subdomain.

There is no public source-to-download, source-to-license, or source-to-retained
use attribution.

## Reddit beta request

On June 24, the maker posted
[[Mac] Looking for beta testers for Locker](https://www.reddit.com/r/software/comments/1uemnda/mac_looking_for_beta_testers_for_locker_a/)
in r/software.

The post:

- Identified Nathan as a solo developer.
- Described an early testing stage.
- Asked for feedback and feature requests.
- Led with on-device behavior and no cloud upload.
- Offered a free lifetime key to motivated testers.
- Directed interested people to comment or send a direct message.

The captured public response showed:

- Score: +1.
- One commenter saying they would like to use it.
- A maker reply saying free keys were on the website and pointing to the disk
  image.

This is direct evidence of one interested person and one maker follow-up. It
does not prove the person downloaded, connected a mailbox, used the product,
or returned.

The current homepage shows five claimed free keys, but there is no source tag
or public statement assigning those claims to Reddit.

### Separate Locker Drop links

The three separate-product links came from:

- `sheepnav.com`, a Chinese tool listing.
- `launches.uicomet.com`, a launch-directory page with a `ref` parameter.
- `stackscope.dev`, a launch listing.

They describe “Locker Drop — encrypted file transfers,” not the email client.
They demonstrate that the maker has seeded another product in launch
directories, but they are not evidence of email-client acquisition.

### Root-domain rows

The ten root-domain rows consisted of:

- Two obvious “buy backlinks / rank first page” anchors with spam scores 70
  and 75.
- Several generic report, share, statistics, or URL-shortener pages with spam
  scores around 50.
- Several links first seen between November 2025 and February 2026, months
  before the June 2026 email-client release.

The sole Wayback homepage capture from July 2025 redirected to a generic
`/lander`. The pre-release rows therefore cannot safely be credited to the
current email product.

No detailed backlink row established:

```text
relevant recommendation
→ current email homepage
→ download or purchase
```

## Content Analysis

The exact-domain request returned one item: an old Portuguese-language
“Inbox.locker” notifications page on `yasteq.com`, fetched in June 2025. It
does not describe Nathan Haring's current Mac email client.

The endpoint did not surface the known Reddit post. This demonstrates a
coverage limitation and is why public search and manual inspection remain
necessary.

## Other narrow checks

Exact-domain searches returned no relevant public result on:

- Product Hunt.
- Hacker News.
- LinkedIn posts.
- X.

The maker's [public portfolio](https://www.nathanharing.com/) establishes his
identity and other projects, but the captured page contained no Locker or
`inbox.locker` reference.

These are narrow negative observations, not proof that a post, private
conversation, deleted page, or unindexed mention never existed.

## Paid and partner evidence

No:

- Affiliate or referral program was found.
- Partner endorsement was found.
- Relevant Google advertiser was returned for `inbox locker`.
- Google ad creative was returned for `inbox.locker`.

The two ad endpoints returned `No Search Results`. This does not rule out other
networks, identities, markets, dates, or unindexed campaigns.

## What appears to matter

The founder post is small but more relevant than the raw backlink count. It
connects:

```text
specific privacy promise
→ request for real testers
→ one visible interested person
→ maker directs them to a free key and download
```

That is not enough evidence to call Reddit an effective acquisition channel.
It is enough to treat problem-specific, disclosed founder participation as a
testable research path.

For Tecotype:

- Enter communities to learn from a concrete mail problem.
- Use original, community-native copy rather than duplicating promotions.
- Tag the source before expanding beyond a small cohort.
- Measure voluntary return after the first value event.
- Exclude spam, domain-history artifacts, and sibling-product links from
  channel conclusions.
